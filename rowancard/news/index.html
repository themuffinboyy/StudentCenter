**RowanCard Mobile App Now Available**

You can now enjoy the convenience of accessing your RowanCard accounts anywhere, anytime. Simply download the eAccounts mobile app to your smartphone device and have all your RowanCard account information at your fingertips. Make a deposit, view transactions, view account balances, and freeze a lost or stolen card anytime, anywhere from your smartphone device.

* In order to make a deposit via your smartphone, you must first complete a deposit on-line and save your payment method.

 

Have you heard about myrowancard.rowan.edu?

Want to add funds to your RowanCard? Want to check your recent transactions or see your balance?  Simply go to: myrowancard.rowan.edu and log in with your Net ID and password.  Parents or relatives can easily add funds to your RowanCard by using the guest log in.  All they need to have is your Rowan ID number and they can add funds to your account any time, any where.  Check out the new site today - myrowancard.rowan.edu !
