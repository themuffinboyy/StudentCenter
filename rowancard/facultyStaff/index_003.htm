Health Insurance

It is required that every student enrolled as a full-time student at a public or private institution of higher education in the state of New Jersey maintain health coverage which provides basic hospital benefits. The coverage shall be maintained throughout the period of the student's enrollment.

Students must purchase health coverage offered by our University at the cost of $120.00 for full year coverage (Aug. 15, 2005 - Aug. 15, 2006) and $72.00 for mid-year coverage (Jan. 15, 2006 - Aug. 15, 2006), unless they can provide evidence of health insurance coverage through another company. The coverage provides major medical benefits up to a maximum of $50,000.00. The premium will be included on the tuition bills of all full-time students. This premium is non-refundable.

Part-Time Students & Dependent Coverage
Coverage is available for part-time students who wish to purchase it. Coverage is also available for dependents of students who have purchased the insurance for themselves. This optional coverage is available directly through Bollinger, Inc. Students need to complete the part-time and dependent insurance enrollment card and mail it to Bollinger with the appropriate payment. This enrollment card may be obtained in the Bursar's Office, Office of the Vice President for Student Affairs or on Bollinger's Website. The university will not be involved in the billing or collection of premiums for this optional coverage.

If you have comparable coverage and wish to waive the College Health Plan, complete the Student Health Insurance Waiver Card and mail it to the Bursar's Office as soon as possible but no later than:

Fall 2005 : September 22, 2005
Spring 2006: February 7, 2006

Cards submitted to the Bursar's Office or postmarked after the deadline will not be accepted.

Please note that this policy will pay in addition to other coverage you may currently have. Therefore, purchasing this plan can help to reduce or eliminate deductibles or co-payments required by your present policy. We urge all students to consider this policy even if you are currently covered by other medical insurance. Visit the Bollinger Website.

The Request for Waiver of Student Medical Insurance
The Request for Waiver of Student Medical Insurance form must be completed if you have insurance elsewhere. Complete and return to the Bursar's Office by fax or mail by the due date in order to be omitted from the medical coverage at Rowan.
